Small Beginnings

My journey into the realm of computer science commenced during my sixth-grade years, sparking an enduring passion for coding and technology. My leadership role within The Birch Wathen Lenox School's Robotics team and my involvement in database development for Aegis Prep, a prominent tutoring company in New York City, significantly honed both my technical skills and unwavering commitment. It was these formative experiences that ultimately propelled me to embark on a journey in computer science at the esteemed University of Wisconsin.

During my tenure at the university, I delved into specialized coursework that laid a robust foundation in computer science. Notably, I immersed myself in subjects such as Computer Graphics, where I mastered the art of visual storytelling and design, and Computer Vision, wherein I explored advanced deep learning techniques for image analysis and recognition. Furthermore, I acquired valuable expertise in Information Security, gaining insights into contemporary data security strategies and effective countermeasures.

My prior engagements, particularly my pivotal role as the lead programmer in the First Robotics Competition, alongside my comprehensive coursework in Computer Graphics, have catalyzed a profound passion for the interdisciplinary domains of artificial intelligence and graphic design. The intricate coding and programming challenges I grappled with while spearheading the robotics team invariably ignited my curiosity about the transformative potential of AI, particularly in automating and augmenting intricate tasks. Simultaneously, my immersive experiences in Computer Graphics unveiled the captivating world of visual narrative and design, serving as a compelling motivation to delve deeper into the intricate principles of graphic design.

This amalgamation of experiences has decisively shaped my ambition to explore the intersection of AI and graphic design. In this convergence, I discern boundless prospects to conceive innovative, visually captivating solutions that can positively influence diverse industries.
